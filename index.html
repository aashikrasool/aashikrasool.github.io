About

Who I am

I'm an AI Research Engineer and PhD candidate at Gachon University, South Korea, specializing in computer vision, image super-resolution, and medical imaging. My work focuses on developing lightweight and efficient deep learning models that can be deployed on resource-constrained devices.

Previously, I worked at Opt-AI.Inc as an AI Research Engineer, designing generalized image enhancement models, performing knowledge distillation, fine-tuning large language models, and working on object detection, NAS, and reinforcement learning.

I have published in venues including IEEE Access, Knowledge-Based Systems (Elsevier), TMLR, Sensors (MDPI), and MICCAI. My research bridges the gap between high-fidelity visual processing and practical real-time deployment for healthcare, environmental monitoring, and edge communication.
